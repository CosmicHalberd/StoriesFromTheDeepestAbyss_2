Forever Below

Hi, I'm Ava. My daddy says I should leave notes behind incase I turn up missing. Right now me and my little sis are resting near a creek near the Hermit's dwelling. It's been a month since we left home to find our mom and dad. So me and my sis decided to go look for them since nobody wanted to look for them.

We heard from a group of adventurers that they saw someone that looked like our mom near the pathway that leads deeper into the dungeon. They said she was with a group of people that looked like adventurers. Daddy went to look for mommy a week ago but he hasn't come back yet. I think he might have gone deeper into the dungeon to look for her.

I don't know what to do. I'm scared but I have to be brave for my little sis. I hope we find mommy and daddy soon. If only the king didn't banish us..
